## üßπ Data Preparation and Modeling (Python)
Raw consumer and transaction data were cleaned, standardized, and reshaped to support analytical rigor.

Key actions included:
- Ensuring data quality through consistency and validation checks
- Handling missing and anomalous values using statistically sound methods
- Engineering features such as customer segments, purchase frequency, and spending indicators
- Structuring datasets for efficient querying and downstream analysis

The output is an analysis-ready dataset suitable for both exploration and reporting.

---

## üßÆ Data Analysis and Business Logic (SQL)
A relational PostgreSQL model was implemented to simulate real-world retail transactions.

The SQL analysis focused on:
- Identifying high-value and repeat customer segments
- Comparing purchasing behavior across demographics and channels
- Evaluating the impact of discounts, reviews, and seasonality on purchasing outcomes
- Understanding how fulfillment and payment preferences influence customer decisions

This layer converts raw transactions into insight that reflects real commercial dynamics.

---

## üìä Visualization and Executive Reporting (Power BI)
An interactive Power BI dashboard was developed to support strategic review and exploratory analysis.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:
- Isolate high-impact customer segments
- Assess revenue concentration and loyalty patterns
- Evaluate promotional effectiveness without over-reliance on discounts
- Explore customer behavior dynamically across multiple dimensions

The design prioritizes clarity, comparability, and speed of interpretation.

---

## üîç Key Insights
- Revenue is concentrated among a limited number of customer segments, highlighting the importance of retention
- Repeat and subscription customers demonstrate meaningfully higher lifetime value
- Discounts influence purchasing behavior selectively and can erode margin if applied broadly
- Customer reviews and delivery experience materially affect purchase completion
- Online and offline channels exhibit distinct behavioral patterns requiring differentiated strategies

---

## üìà Strategic Recommendations
- Shift marketing focus from broad acquisition to targeted retention of high-value segments
- Refine discount strategies to balance conversion impact and profitability
- Use behavioral signals to personalize engagement rather than relying on demographic averages
- Align product assortment and logistics decisions with customer expectations revealed in the data
